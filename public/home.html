why a personal website?


web 1.0 feels more natural to me. everyone had a personal website back then. all hand coded, made with passion. it felt more human. everything now is about making your website look mobile friendly, or to be minimalistic. this is the exact opposite of what i want. i want my website to be open source. i want my website to be unique to me. back then, websites were an "anything goes" type deal, but now theres so many rules to follow and its bullshit. so i want this to inspire other people to create their own personal websites. filled with journals, blinkies, deviant art stamps, shrines, autoplay music, just to piss off people who lick the boots of big tech. so please, go make yourself a neocities account if you havent yet, and learn html because its totally worth it.
